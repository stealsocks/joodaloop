Aphorisms

Pithy sayings and accidental epigrams. If you want collections of better ones, you should check out this (Gwern) and this (Bret Victor)

One must imagine Sisyphus being cheered on.

To a guy who's a nail, everything seems like a hammer.

Boldness is measured in distance from death.

As far as I can tell, you are what you fight for.

Make everything as easy as possible, but no easier.

Do what you can, and then just a little bit more.

A broken clock .

Know thyself place.

Balance precedes falling.

You can either fear God, or death. But never both at the same time.
